In Minecraft, farming is a great way to get food because in Minecraft when pigs die, they don't respawn making food  hard to find. So you need a farm. To build a basic farm just hoe a piece of dirt and plant seeds in it. In a few days the plant will have a brown top on it. break it and you get 1-3 seeds and 1 wheat. Use this for cookies and more!
By the way, you can also plant melon seeds and pumpkin seeds. If you plant melon or pumpkin seeds, leave a block with nothing on it next to it or it WON'T GROW.

(Note potatoes and carrots grow similarly to wheat)
